Loma ("we", "our", "us") operates the Loma Chrome extension and API service. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 1. What We Collect

### 1.1 Text You Submit
When you use Loma to rewrite text, we send your input text to our server for processing. We store:
- The input text and rewritten output
- Detected intent and language metadata
- Platform context (e.g., "gmail", "slack")
- Timestamp and response time

We use this data to improve our rewriting quality and intent detection.

### 1.2 Account Information
If you create an account (via Google Sign-in), we store:
- Your email address
- Authentication provider
- Subscription tier and usage counts

### 1.3 Usage Analytics
We collect anonymized usage events:
- Rewrite count, tone changes, intent selections
- Error events
- Platform distribution

We do **not** collect browsing history, page content beyond text fields you actively submit, or any data from pages where you do not use Loma.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- **Provide the service:** Process your rewrite requests via our API
- **Improve quality:** Analyze rewrite patterns to improve intent detection and output quality
- **Enforce limits:** Track usage for free tier and billing
- **Communicate:** Send service-related notifications (no marketing emails without consent)

## 3. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your data. We share data only with:
- **Anthropic (Claude API):** Your input text is sent to Claude for rewriting. Anthropic's usage policy applies.
- **Supabase:** Our database provider, for storing user accounts and rewrite history.
- **Stripe / PayOS:** For processing payments (billing data only, not your text).

## 4. Data Retention

- **Rewrite history:** Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
- **Account data:** Retained while your account is active
- **Analytics events:** Retained for 12 months in aggregated form

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data (email privacy@loma.app)
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Export** your rewrite history
- **Opt out** of analytics (disable in extension settings)

## 6. Security

- All data transmitted over HTTPS
- API keys and tokens stored encrypted
- Database access restricted with row-level security
- No text data stored in browser local storage (only settings and counts)

## 7. Children

Loma is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

## 8. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via the extension or email.

## 9. Contact
