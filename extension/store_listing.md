# Chrome Web Store Listing — Loma

## Short Description (132 chars max)

Rewrite Vietnamese emails into professional English. AI that understands Vietnamese work culture, not just translation.

## Detailed Description

Loma helps Vietnamese professionals write better English emails, messages, and documents — without sounding like a translation tool.

**The problem:** Vietnamese professionals code-switch, use cultural politeness markers, and rely on hierarchical language patterns that generic AI tools don't understand. ChatGPT and Grammarly treat these as errors instead of cultural context.

**How Loma is different:**
- Understands Vietnamese work culture: triple apologies, kinship pronouns (anh/chị/em), face-saving language
- Detects 15 intent types: payment requests, follow-ups, disagreements, escalations, feedback, and more
- Preserves your names, numbers, dates, and identifiers
- Works on Gmail, Outlook, Slack, Teams, Google Docs, Jira, Notion, LinkedIn, GitHub
- Supports 4 tones: Professional, Direct, Warm, Formal
- Vietnamese output mode for công văn, reports, and proposals

**Features:**
- One-click rewrite button on any text field
- Keyboard shortcut: Ctrl+Shift+. (Cmd+Shift+. on Mac)
- Free tier: 5 rewrites/day
- Pay-as-you-go: 20 or 100 credits
- Pro: unlimited rewrites
- Privacy-first: your text is processed and never stored for training

Built for Vietnamese professionals who want to communicate clearly in English while respecting their cultural context.
